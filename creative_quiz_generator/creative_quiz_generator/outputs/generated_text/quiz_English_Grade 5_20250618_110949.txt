Write a Short Answer quiz for Grade 5 on English grammar or literature. 4 questions. none of the questions should be more than 1 sentence long.

1. What is the past tense of the verb "run"?
Answer: The past tense of the verb "run" is "ran".
2. Who wrote the novel "Charlotte's Web"?
Answer: E.B. White wrote the novel "Charlotte's Web".
3. What is the noun form of the adjective "happy"?
Answer: The noun form of the adjective "happy" is "happiness".
4. Which punctuation mark is used to indicate a question?
Answer: The punctuation mark used to indicate a question is a question mark (?).
